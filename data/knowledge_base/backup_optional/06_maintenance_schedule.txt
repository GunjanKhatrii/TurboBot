WIND TURBINE COMPREHENSIVE MAINTENANCE SCHEDULE
================================================

INTRODUCTION
============
This document provides a complete maintenance schedule for wind turbines, from daily monitoring to major overhauls. Following this schedule maximizes availability, extends component life, and prevents costly failures.

MAINTENANCE PHILOSOPHY
======================

Three-Tier Approach:

Tier 1: Condition Monitoring (Continuous)
- Automated SCADA monitoring
- Remote diagnostics
- Trend analysis
- Early warning detection

Tier 2: Preventive Maintenance (Scheduled)
- Time-based interventions
- Inspections
- Replacements
- Adjustments

Tier 3: Corrective Maintenance (As Needed)
- Repairs
- Component replacements
- Emergency interventions
- Upgrades

DAILY MAINTENANCE (AUTOMATED)
==============================

Performed by: SCADA System (automatic)
Duration: Continuous
Cost: Included in monitoring system

Monitored Parameters:

1. Power Performance:
   - Power output vs. wind speed
   - Capacity factor
   - Availability
   - Energy production

2. Temperatures:
   - Gearbox oil: <65°C normal
   - Generator bearings: <70°C normal
   - Generator windings: <110°C normal
   - Ambient temperature

3. Vibration Levels:
   - Main bearing
   - Gearbox
   - Generator
   - Alarm if >3.5 mm/s

4. Oil Condition:
   - Pressure: 1.5-2.5 bar
   - Temperature: 40-65°C
   - Level: Within normal range

5. System Status:
   - No active alarms
   - All safety systems operational
   - Communication links active
   - No error codes

Automated Actions:
- Log all parameters
- Send alerts for anomalies
- Generate daily reports
- Track availability

CONDITION-BASED MAINTENANCE TRIGGERS
=====================================

Beyond scheduled maintenance, immediate action required for:

Temperature Alarms:
- Gearbox >70°C: Inspect within 48h
- Generator >110°C: Inspect within 24h
- Any bearing >10°C above normal: Investigate immediately

Vibration Alarms:
- >4.0 mm/s: Inspect within 1 week
- >7.0 mm/s: Inspect within 48h
- Rapid increase (>50% in 1 week): Immediate inspection

Oil Analysis:
- Iron >200 ppm: Schedule inspection
- Water >1000 ppm: Schedule oil change
- Acid number >4.0: Immediate oil change

Performance:
- Power curve deviation >10%: Investigate within 2 weeks
- Availability <95%: Review immediately
- Excessive alarms/stops: Immediate review
